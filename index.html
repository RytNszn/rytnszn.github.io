Ryota Nishizono

Research Specialist @ NTT CS Labs

About Me

I am a research specialist with a focus on cognitive neuroscience, particularly interested in the dynamics of human cognition and psychological states during natural tasks. I am currently working for Kashino Diverse Brain Research Laboratory at NTT Communication Science Laboratories

In my 20s, I was a professional cyclist and was active in international competitions.
